Rules
Base Rules

Base rules from previous seasons are still in effect as follows:

  • A win will award 3 points to the leader
  • A 2nd, 3rd or 4th place finish will award 2 points to the leader
  • A finish from 5th - 10th will award 1 point to the leader
  • Anything outside 10th place will award 0 points
  • MVP Rules
    • After a game that finished above top 5 a MVP roll will occur. The chance of MVP for the game is as follows:
      • First - 100%
      • 2nd - 80%
      • 3rd - 60%
      • 4th - 40%
      • 5th - 20%
    • Players will all submit MVP votes silently to the leader of the game. Once all players have voted the result will be calculated as follows:
      • If all players received a vote no players will receive points (wash)
      • If 2 players tied for most votes they will each receive 1 point
      • If 1 player received the most votes they will receive 2 points
    • Any players (excluding the leader and MVP) that voted for the MVP will receive 1 point for predicting correctly
  • If another player is rebooted (by a van) all players currently alive are awarded 1 point
Perks
  • Players will receive 1 perk point at the start of the season
    (choose wisely!)
  • Players will receive add additional perk point when team wins hit 3 / 5 / 7 / 10 / 15 / 20
  • Player will receive 1 respec point for every win the team earns during the season
  • Perks may only be selected and changed at the beginning of a play night
Last Game

The last game of the night will follow special rules according to a theme. For now the only theme will be last man standing

  • Points will be awarded base on how long each player survives
    • Game win - Alive players receive 3 points
    • Last alive - 2 points
    • 2nd/3rd - 1 point
    • First to die - 0 points
  • Players may drop anywhere they wish (together or separate)
  • If another player is rebooted (by a van) all players currently alive are awarded 1 point
