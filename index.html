Rules
The Wheel

Things are shaking up this season! At the beginning of the night the wheel will have the following structure:

  • One slot for each player (Standard Lead)
  • One slot for 2v2 Spree (Last Place Choice)
  • One slot for 2v2 Spree (Random Partners)

Wheel will carry it's state over to future nights and is only reloaded when every slot has been removed.

Revives

The following are in effect for all game modes

  • There must be at least 2 players alive for revive points to count
  • Reboot Vans
    • If another player is rebooted (by a van) all players currently alive are awarded 1 point
  • Knocks
    • Players that assist in picking up a knocked player (have the lifting animation) will be awarded 1 point
    • If multiple players are knocked, those players aren't eligible for revive points until all non-card players are standing
NEW
Standard Lead

At the end of a standard lead points are awarded (incentivizing exciting aggressive leads):

  • 4 kills - 1 point
  • 8 kills - 2 points
  • 12 kills - 3 points
  • 16 kills - 4 points
  • Wins award 2 points bonus on top of any of the above tiers

Dominator

  • If a player (non-leader) performs more eliminations then the sum of the other team members they earn 1 point at the end of the game

Leaders are exempt from earning Dominator or Revive points during a game

NEW
2v2 Spree
  • Teams are either random or picked based on wheel. Points will be awarded base on which team has the most collective kills in a game.
    • 1st - 2 points
    • Lowest kills - 0 points
  • Resurrection of the opposing team will award points to the players on the opposite team.
  • Players may drop anywhere they wish (together or separate). Ties will award the first place points to both players.
  • DOUBLE DOWN If all parties agree after team selection the game is worth double points to the winners
NEW
MVP

MVP votes will occur in any game with one of the following:

  • 10 Team Kills
  • Top 5 Finish
  • Game Length of 15min+

Leaders cannot be selected in MVP voting

Players will all submit MVP votes silently to BrenBot. Once all players have voted the result will be calculated as follows:

  • If all players received a vote no players will receive points (wash)
  • If 2 players tied for most votes they will each receive 1 point
  • If 1 player received the most votes they will receive 2 points
  • Any players (excluding the leader and MVP) that voted for a solo MVP will receive 1 point for predicting correctly

Leaders must also award a leadership point to other team members. Players that earn a leadership award earn 1 point if they were not voted as MVP for the game.

Discussions during gameplay are allowed but once the game has ended discussing around the MVP is forbidden.

NEW
Seasonal Records

Records start at 0 for this season, and every time a record is broken it gives the player 1 point

For the first wheel of the season records will be calculated at the end of the wheel sequence

Records include:

  • Team kills (awarded to leader)
  • Individual kills (awarded to player)
  • 2v2 Team kills (awarded to each player on a team)
NEW
Prestige Token

Any player voted for MVP in a winning game will receive 1 Prestige Token

Uses include:

  • You may pick or override partner choice for 2v2 spree
  • Can FORCE a 2vs2 Double Down without others agreeing
  • Can choose to have your lead based on Kills or Placement (instead of Lead vote)
